2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register for and use Skills United, we collect information that you provide to us directly, including:

  • Full name

  • Email address

When you use the App, we and our service providers may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Date and time of access
  • Firebase Analytics data (aggregated usage statistics)
  • Crash reports and error logs

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

If you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive your name and email address from Google. We do not receive your Google password. The information we receive from Google is governed by Google's Privacy Policy in addition to this Privacy Policy.

2.4 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Your precise real-time location or GPS data

  • Your camera roll or photos (unless you upload a profile picture, if this feature is enabled)

  • Government-issued identification documents

How We Store and Protect Your Information

4.1 Storage Location and Infrastructure

Skills United uses Google Firebase to store and process your personal information. Firebase services used include:

  • Firebase Authentication — for account creation and login
  • Cloud Firestore — for storing your profile, skills, messages, and other App data
  • Firebase Storage — for storing any uploaded files or images
  • Firebase Analytics — for aggregated, anonymised usage statistics

By using the App, you acknowledge and consent that your personal information may be stored on servers located outside of Australia, including in the United States and other countries where Google operates data centres. We rely on Google's Standard Contractual Clauses and other appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted storage of passwords — we never store your password in plain text
  • Firebase Security Rules to restrict access to your data
  • Optional two-factor authentication for your account
  • Code obfuscation in the App's release build
  • Input validation and sanitisation to prevent injection attacks

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4.3 Data Retention

  • Account information — retained for the duration of your account and for a reasonable period after deletion to comply with legal obligations
  • Messages — retained in our database for the duration of your account; deleted upon account deletion subject to any legal holds
  • User reports — retained for a period necessary to investigate and resolve reports and to defend any legal claims
  • Technical logs — retained for a limited period for security and diagnostic purposes
  • Anonymised analytics data — may be retained indefinitely

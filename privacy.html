How We Store and Protect Your Information 4.1 Storage Location and Infrastructure Skills United uses Google Firebase to store and process your personal information. Firebase services used include: • Firebase Authentication — for account creation and login • Cloud Firestore — for storing your profile, skills, messages, and other App data • Firebase Storage — for storing any uploaded files or images • Firebase Analytics — for aggregated, anonymised usage statistics By using the App, you acknowledge and consent that your personal information may be stored on servers located outside of Australia, including in the United States and other countries where Google operates data centres. We rely on Google's Standard Contractual Clauses and other appropriate safeguards for international data transfers. 4.2 Security Measures We implement reasonable technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information, including: • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS • Encrypted storage of passwords — we never store your password in plain text • Firebase Security Rules to restrict access to your data • Optional two-factor authentication for your account • Code obfuscation in the App's release build • Input validation and sanitisation to prevent injection attacks However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You use the App at your own risk and we are not responsible for any unauthorised access to your account that results from your failure to protect your login credentials. 4.3 Data Retention We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. Specifically: • Account information — retained for the duration of your account and for a reasonable period after deletion to comply with legal obligations • Messages — retained in our database for the duration of your account; deleted upon account deletion subject to any legal holds • User reports — retained for a period necessary to investigate and resolve reports and to defend any legal claims • Technical logs — retained for a limited period for security and diagnostic purposes • Anonymised analytics data — may be retained indefinitely 5.
